Data Centers and Their Climate Impact

America’s data center boom is reshaping the grid

As interest in AI and LLMs grow, an abundance of data centers are being built, raising concerns for their impacts on resource consumption and the environment.

They are spreading quickly across the country, and the impact is showing up in power demand, carbon emissions, local utility bills, and energy consumption. Our visualization maps where facilities are concentrated and estimates the potential impacts of that growth across the US.
